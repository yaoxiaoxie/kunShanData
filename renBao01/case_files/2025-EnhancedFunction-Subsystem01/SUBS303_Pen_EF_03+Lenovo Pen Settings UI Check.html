Eraser and Tip sensitivity are controlled by sliders in the control panel. This applies a curve to the pressure data to modify the values reported to the system. Softer settings will report higher pressure with less force used, Firmer settings will require more force on the pen tip to report higher pressure levels. Tilt Sensitivity can be modified to provide higher or lower tilt values to the system on Pen sensors that have support for tilt.
