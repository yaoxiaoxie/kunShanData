Section : Windows image verification test Attachment :
Workloading : 10(Min) LeadingTime : 10(Min) Phase : Test Log Mandatory : N
1.Open "Settings-> Apps->Installed apps" to find Dolby Access and Dolby Vision.
Dolby Access and Dolby Vision app could be found.
N
2.Open Dolby Access.
The HOME tab is the default landing page when you launch Dolby Access.
N
3.Check the "Dolby Vision" status in PRODUCTS tab.
The Dolby Vision status show"Ready to use".
N
4. Play test video through Microsoft's built-in video player (Movies & TV app or Media Player app)
"Dolby Vision" logo will appear in the video for a few seconds at first. Note: 1.Please take pictures or videos by original camera with mobile phone for test result. 2.You can refer to attachment "pass_fail" to find more information.
Y
5.Check the "DOLBY VISION" status in SETTINGS tab.
You will see three mode "Bright","Dark","Vivid" in DOLBY VISION SETTINGS. The default status is "Bright".
N
6.Select one of the three available modes e.g: "Bright".
N
7.Observe the change in the video playing in the Movies & TV app or Media Player app for brightness level.
The brightness level should be increased in the Movies & TV app or Media Player app.
N
8. Change the mode to "Dolby Vision Dark".
The brightness level should be reduced and the video will look darker.
N
9.Change the mode to "Dolby Vision Vivid".
Dolby Vision Vivid is the brightest mode.
N
Note: 1.Windows Media Player and Internet Explorer are not compatible with the Dolby Vision codec. Third party Win32 based media players are not compatible with Dolby Vision. 2.On dolby vision v1.3.77.0 or later, if you are not playing the videos, the picture mode may not be changed.
N

Section : Compatibility test Attachment :
Workloading : 20(Min) LeadingTime : 20(Min) Phase : Test Log Mandatory : N
1.Enter Dolby Vision interface, play test video through Microsoft's built-in video player (Movies & TV app or Media Player app)
N
2.Set PC enter S3(MS) five times.
N
3.Wake PC up and check Movies & TV app or Media Player app.
System should wake up normally,the video can play on Movies & TV app or Media Player app without any artifacts or garbage. Note: If the Movies & TV app or Media Player app is in a pause condition, press Play to resume playing the content.Playback should resume playing without artifacts.
N
4.Open Edge browser and log on to Netfilx website,choose a dolby vision video to play,such as "The Chef's Table".
N
5.Set PC enter S4 five times.
N
6.After 15–20 seconds, press the power button to wake the device.
System should wake up normally,the video can play on Netfilx website without any artifacts or garbage. Note: If Netflix is in a pause condition, press Play to resume playing the content. The playback should resume playing without artifacts.
N
7. After playback resumes, switch between Dolby Vision Dark, Dolby Vision Bright, and Dolby Vision Vivid picture modes through the Dolby Vision interface.
The brightness level change should be observed when switching picture modes.
N
8.If units support ACM(Auto Color Management) function,please test with ACM on,repeat 1-7 step.If not,please skip.
System should wake up normally without any artifacts or garbage.
N
