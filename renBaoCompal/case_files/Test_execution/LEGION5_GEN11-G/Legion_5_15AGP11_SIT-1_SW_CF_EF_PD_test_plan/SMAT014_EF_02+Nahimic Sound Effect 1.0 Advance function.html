Section : Nahimic GUI check (110) Attachment :
Workloading : (Min) LeadingTime : (Min) Phase :
Easy Surround
Notes 1: Please cover internal speaker and 3.5mm jack external speaker for Easy Surround Front speaker test.
Notes 2: Please test with music/video/game that support 5.1/7.1 channel.
1.Click On/Off button to turn on Easy Surround, follow guide setup
Easy Surround can be enabled.Make sure you can successfully set up Easy Surround with the guide
1.1Connect a Bluetooth speaker(You can skip this step if you already connect)
Click 'settings/Bluetooth' to open Bluetooth&other device page
1.2Place the connected BT speaker behind you a few meters away.
1.3Select the front speakers you will use
Internal speakers or jack external speakers(please connect first) will show in the drop-down box
1.4Select the rear speaker you will use: a connected Bluetooth speaker
1.5Synchronize the sound between your front and rear speakers
Adjust the slider until you hear the test sound coming from both the speakers at the same time
2.Once the setup is done, Easy Surround page will be displayed
2.1Check Easy Surround display icon
It shows Speakers.
2.2Drag or click to change the volume of Speakers
Both the selected speakers volume will change as you adjust
2.3Click Mute button several times
Both the selected speakers will Mute and unmute
2.4Click ‘Read More’ button
Nahimic will pop up guide page
2.5Click ‘Setup’ button
Nahimic will pop up Setup page
2.6Click ‘Adjust’ button
Nahimic will pop up sync up your speakers page
2.7Click try button，Launch an audio sample that allows to test Easy Surround
You will be able to feel the surround sound.You can adjust the Settings(front volume/rear volume/delay/front speakers/rear speakers) directly on the page
2.8Click On/Off button to turn off Easy Surround
Easy Surround can be disabled.
2.9Click On/Off button to turn on Easy Surround
Easy Surround can be enabled.The last settings will be remembered
5.1 or 7.1 channels setting methods: Choose 5.1 or 7.1 channels in game Audio speaker settings
3.Playing video games with Easy Surround
You will be able to feel the surround sound.Ensure sound output from both speakers normally
Suggested video player: Potplayer；5.1 or 7.1 channels setting methods: Right click Potplayer->Audio->Output speaker->choose 5.1 or 7.1 channels
4.Watching a movie with Easy Surround
You will be able to feel the surround sound.Ensure sound output from both speakers normally
Sound Tracker
Notes:The use of Sound Tracker feature needs the Sound Tracker Engine to be running. You can enable it in the Settings page.
1.Click On/Off button to turn on/off Sound Tracker.
The sound tracker can be enabled/disabled.
2.Customize and click the hotkey to turn on/off Sound Tracker.
The sound tracker can be enabled/disabled.
3.Click Try button.
Launch an audio sample allowing you to preview how the radar will react in your game.
4.Drag or click the Sound Tracker.
The Sound Tracker will be placed at wherever you want on your screen.
5.Click the colored circle to open the color edition window.
You can apply the color you want to the dynamic segments.
6.Drag Sound Tracker to a suitable position, then open the suggested game
In the game, Sound Tracker is shown on the suitable position that tester drags to,and it can't be click and drag.
7. Press Alt+Tab to go to Nahimic Sound Tracker tab, then drag Sound Tracker to another suitable position, go back to game.
In the game , Sound Tracker is shown on the suitable position that tester drags to.
8. Press Alt+Tab to go to Nahimic Sound Tracker tab, change size/transparency/color, then go back to game.
In the game, Sound Tracker is shown correct as the tester adjusts.
9. Start game, check Sound Tracker.
When the game sounds are output from different angle and position,sound will be shown on Sound Tracker as the color jump in right angle.
10. When in game, press Customized hotkey.
Sound Tracker will disappear.
11. Press Customized hotkey again.
Sound Tracker will appear on game with correct setting.
Sound Sharing
Notes: Connect and optimize headphones in Audio Tab before adding them to the feature or else no Nahimic effects on devices
1.Connect and pair 2 Bluetooth headphones.
2.Click On/Off button to turn on Sound Sharing Plus.
The first selection device windows appear.
3.Select the first device.
Once the first device is selected,a new window will automatically appear for the second device selection.
4.Select the second device.
The second device can be selected successfully.
5.Playback the music file.
The music will output from 2 selected device simultaneously.
6.Drag or click the volume bar of each headphone.
Each headphone's own volume level can be adjusted.
7.Click the mute button of each headphone.
Each headphone can be separately muted.
8.Click On/Off button to turn off Sound Sharing.
The "select your audio playback device" window appear.
9.Choose the audio device you want your sound to be back in the list.
The music will output from the device you selected.
10.Connect 2 USB headphones.Reselect first & second device, repeat step5-step9
11.Connect Bluetooth+jack headphones.Reselect first & second device, repeat step5-step9
12.Connect Bluetooth+USB headphones.Reselect first & second device, repeat step5-step9
13.Connect USB+jack headphones.Reselect first & second device, repeat step5-step9
Streaming Dashboard
1.The first time open Streaming tab will pop up Onboarding Guide, follow guide setup
Onboarding Guide will pop up again by clicking 'Help' icon.
2.Click On/Off button to turn on Streaming Dashboard, Open music/video/game with different audio streams(Apps/Games such as Media Player/Microsoft Edge/etc...)
Apps/Games will show in the list on the left.
3.Install and open OBS Studio tool
Select 'I will only be using the virtual camera'
4.Click Controls—Settings in the lower right corner
Select Audio—Gobal audio devices—Desktop audio—Nahimic VAD, Click OK.(Nahimic VAD device can only be found when this feature is turned on)
5.Mute Microphone in the Audio Mixer
6.Click Controls—Start Recording in the lower right corner

Notes: when Streaming Dashboard enable, selected Apps/Games sound will be decreased a bit due to Nahimic effects.
7.Click the + icon to select the desired Apps/Games you want to put in the streaming section in Nahimic Streaming Dashboard
You can adjust the volume of each audio stream you selected(cover Max and Min), The volume is synced with Windows Volume Mixer.
8.Click the x icon to select the Apps/Games you want to remove in Nahimic Streaming Dashboard
Apps/Games will be removed in the list on the right.
9.Click Controls—Stop Recording in the lower right corner(OBS Studio tool)
Recording file will be saved to the default Videos path.
10.Play Recording file, Make sure the audio streaming is recorded normally
Apps/Games audio streaming you selected on the right will be recorded, the others audio streaming will not.
11.Click On/Off button to turn off Streaming Dashboard
Make sure Streaming Dashboard is disabled. Nahimic VAD device can not be found in OBS Studio settings.
12.Turn on Streaming Dashboard again, Nahimic will remember the last settings.
